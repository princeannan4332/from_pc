Auntie Mary was affable,exceptionally reliable and very accomodating.She supported all that appeared to her to be a just cause. Her tougthfulness and selflessness always drove her on visit to family members to ascertain thier well being. Undoubtedly therefore we as a family will miss Sister Ayikailey No.1 .We will miss her sound advices and counsel to both young and old. We willl miss her generosity and kindheartedness.We like to express the fond hope at this time that Auntie Mary's death would help reawaken the spirit of unity in the familyand generate a sense of belonging among us all.
